Louie Dowling Men's Fellowship

Our Louie Dowling men's fellowship meets on the third Thursday of every month. We meet at 7:00 PM in the fellowship hall. The program consists of a meal, a short business session, and a devotional. We take up a donation which allows us to pay for our two main projects. Projects are Ladies night out where we take all of our unescorted ladies to a nice evening meal once a year and pay for six Christmas boxes to be sent to children around the world.
