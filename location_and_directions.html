From Hartsville

  1. Go South on State 403 to the Oates Community.
  2. Turn Left on E 7 Pines Road. (There is a closed brick building on the left)
  3. Go 1/10th of a mile and turn RIGHT on Philadelphia Street.
  4. Follow this road about 3.8 miles.
  5. The church is on the right. (Look for the white fence in front)

From Lamar

  1. Go East on US 401.
  2. As soon as you cross over I-20, turn left on the first road, Lawson Grove Road.
  3. Go 2.4 miles to the only Stop Sign you will come to.
  4. Turn RIGHT on Philadelphia Street.
  5. Go 7/10ths of a mile, and you will see the church on the right.

From Florence (Option 1)

  1. Get on Interstate I-20 heading West.
  2. Come off at Exit 131.
  3. Turn Right and immediately (100 yards) turn Left on Lawson Grove Road.
  4. Go 2.4 miles to the only Stop Sign you will come to.
  5. Turn RIGHT on Philadelphia Street.
  6. Go 7/10ths of a mile, and you will see the church on the right.

From Florence (Option 2)

  1. Go North on Hoffmeyer Rd to the traffic circle and turn left.
  2. Go 1.1 miles and Philadelphia Rd angles off to the right.
  3. Go 2.7 miles and the church is on the left.

From Bishopville

  1. Get on Interstate I-20 heading East.
  2. Come off at Exit 131.
  3. Turn LEFT and cross over the Interstate.
  4. As soon as you cross over I-20, turn left on the first road, Lawson Grove Road.
  5. Go 2.4 miles to the only Stop Sign you will come to.
  6. Turn RIGHT on Philadelphia Street.
  7. Go 7/10ths of a mile, and you will see the church on the right.
